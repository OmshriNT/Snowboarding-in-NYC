Snowboarding in the streets of NYC on a winter stormy day.

Casey Neistat, an awesome YouTuber and filmmaker took the internet by storm. On Jan 23, 2016, Casey decides to go on snowboarding pulled by a custom-built car which was specially designed to run on the snow-covered roads.

The dangerous stunt Casey pulled that day was certainly illegal. The NYPD police cars couldn't keep up with the Jeep, because of the weather condition. This became a preferable condition for people who are looking for an adventurous day. Going up and down must have felt really good.

For the climax, Casey uplifted the American flag in the New York Times Square. The people even cheered for him as he swift away. Watch the whole video of Casey Neistat snowboarding in NYC.
